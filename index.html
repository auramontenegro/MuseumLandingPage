The WH-Moe presents: BABYLON - a collaborative exhibition

10th November 2022

A showcase of the extraordinary art created as a result of the interactions between kings, diplomats and merchants. This unprecedented movement of luxury goods and precious materials lead to the transformation of the visual arts in the ancient Near East and the Eastern Mediterranean during the second millenium B.C..

Approximately 320 objects from temples, palaces and tombs will give a sense of the artisitic exchange of the period. Free entry

Museum awarded £150,000 Grant to help with digital transformation

5th November 2022

The Friends of the WH-Moe is delighted to announce that it has been awarded a grant of £150.000 from the Digital Innovation and Engagement Fund to support the museum with the development of its digital presence.

The fund is product of the collaboration between UK Research and Innovation (UKRI), the Arts and Humanities Research Council (AHRC) and the Museums Association.
